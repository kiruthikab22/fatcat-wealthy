MBA Department Manager

Mid-Level Madurai

Primary Responsibility:

The Department Manager will be responsible for developing and implementing strategic plans aligned with organizational objectives while overseeing the daily operations of the department to ensure efficiency and productivity.

The role involves leading, mentoring, and evaluating team members to achieve performance targets, maintain high-quality standards, monitor budgets, analyze key performance indicators, prepare reports for senior leadership, and identify opportunities for business growth and process improvement.

Job Specification:

  • MBA in Business Administration / Finance / Marketing / Operations
  • 3–5 years of managerial or supervisory experience
  • Strong leadership and decision-making abilities
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Knowledge of financial planning and business strategy
  • Proficiency in MS Office and business tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving mindset
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks under pressure

Employment Type: Full-time

Work place Type: Hybrid

Salary: Commensurate with experience and skills

Experience Required: Minimum 3 Years
